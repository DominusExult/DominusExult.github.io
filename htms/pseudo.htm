I was always very interested in science-fiction novels. Especially those which deal with timetravel, timemachines and such things. ´It´s always interesting how the authors think about timetravel and if they really think about all the consequences, paradoxes...   That interest lead me to think in similar lines.

Let´s consider there is a kind of timemachine, it´s not that sophisticated, that you are bodily able to travel in time, but you are able to travel in your mind. That means you can send your mind back in time into your own body, say five years before. Now you know the consequences of all the things you chose to do and you are able to do it again, make it better or not to do it at all. Would you call this girl, make a date, knowing that some time later she would break your heart ? Probably not. But if you avoided all your failures what kind of person would you become? You wouldn´t be the same as you didn´t really learn to handle mistakes, your character wouldn´t be the same, most likely you wouldn´t be a nice person.

So to what am I leading you ? I don´t really know. Probably I want to tell you that life is not a computer game in which you are able to load an old game when you made a mistake. Though a lot of times you may wish to do so and start again. Hm, now I lost my own thought in this. There´s more to come.
